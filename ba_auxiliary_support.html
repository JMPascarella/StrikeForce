Commander Dante is one of the most legendary Space Marine Commanders in the Imperium. He has led the Blood Angels Chapter for over 1,000 years and it is widely believed that he is the oldest living Space Marine. No-one within the Imperium has fought in as many war zones as Dante and few can match him for the number of foes he has killed. Amongst Space Marines, Dante is regarded as a paragon of the Space Marine's cause to defend humanity. Amongst mortal men he is nothing less than a saviour, a golden god who descends from the heavens on wings of fire to smite his foes in deadly close combat.

The Sanguinor is a golden angel of vengeance who descends from the heavens only in times of the Blood Angels' greatest need. To most he is a myth, but those few who have seen him cut a bloody path through the Blood Angels' enemies know different.
Rising up from trails of elaborate scrolls, the Sanguinor is an astonishingly detailed miniature. With two angel wings stretched out wide, which is mirrored in the pose of his arms, the Sanguinor carries a grail in one hand and a sword featuring a hilt in the style of the Blood Angels icon in the other. Winged details can be found across its muscular torso, laurel wreath motifs on the knees of the armour, as well as gemstones, metal studs and an iron halo set between his wings. His shoulder pads contain raised, sculpted details of the Blood Angels iconography.

Mephiston, Lord of Death, has risen swiftly through the ranks of the Blood Angels and now holds the office of Chief Librarian. He is a figure of awe and reverence to most of his Battle-Brothers, who perceive him as a saviour in these times of woe.
The highly detailed Mephiston model contains a number of fascinating features that mark him out in any Warhammer 40,000 collection. He wears a flowing robe and cloak, lavish artificer armour, and a psychic hood topped with horned skulls. His belt is detailed with gems in the shape of blood drops, and also features two syringes. There is an immense skull on one shoulder pad and Blood Angels iconography on the other. Skulls also feature along his cloak as well as in the centre of his chest. He also carries a plasma pistol and an immense force sword.

Chaplain Lemartes is the warden of the Death Company and never have they been such a potent force as under his guidance. Though Lemartes himself is afflicted by the accursed Black Rage, his formidable willpower has allowed him to remain in control of his actions, leaving him free to lead the Death Company in glorious charges that eclipse all the deeds of legend.
Lemartes stands atop a rocky base in a dynamic pose with his bolt pistol in one hand and the Crozius Arcanum in another. His armour is full of details including purity seals, scrolls, Blood Angels iconography, chalice imagery, as well as an immense skull on one shoulder pad. His jump pack, too, is plastered with similar features. A halo encapsulates his skull head, and he has boned feet protruding from beneath his riveted armour.
