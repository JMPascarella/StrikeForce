Blood Angels vs. Death Guard

2,000 Points

Visions of Victory (CA18)

Loss; concede

Arriving at their shared destination ahead of the rest of the detachment, the Blood Angels deployed upon Planet Kraxxus, holding much of their force in Deep Strike reserve. This proved to be a mortal mistake as initial auspex scans showed the chosen deployment zone to be largely vacant of the defending Death Guard forces but in reality they were shrouded by the combined forces of two daemon princes with a corrupted Imperial Knight for support. By the time deep strike forces arrived upon the battlefield, the initial landing force had been largely destroyed. After a few more fatal blows dealt to the Wings of the Archangel, the Archangel himself, First Captain Karlaen, ordered a full retreat back to the Divinitatem Sublimius. Covering their retreat, Brother Librarian Loredon, entombed within his dreadnought, was able to single-handedly slay one of the two commanding daemon princes. As per First Captain Karlaen, chapter serfs are being dispatched to Brother Librarian Loredon to record this heroic deed on his chassis and telepathic communication has been dispatched to Baal to update the Hall of Rememberance as well.
